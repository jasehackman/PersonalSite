About Me

I have always been a strange mix of creative and analytical, but have struggled to find where that duality can coexist. As a musician, I've been able to utilize the creative part of me to write and play music but I've never quite fit in with other musicians in my desire to add structure and order to the creative process. In my business ventures, I have been able to live in details and data, but have felt unable to express my creative side in a way that is fulfilling. I have found the perfect union of the two in programming.

As a software developer, I am able to use my whole self to solve problems. I have the opportunity to step back, look at the big picture and come up with a creative solution and at the same time, dive deep into the details in order to bring that solution to life. I enjoy the new challenges that programming brings me every day. I look forward to the consistent opportunity and necessity to learn new things. My favorite aspect of programming is the ability to use code to solve real problems in the world around me.

When not writing code you can find me singing and playing guitar in venues around the Nashville area.

Projects

Song-Share

Song-Share is a single page responsive CRUD web application built with React.js and Bootstrap that reduces the barriers between publishers and songwriters. Song-Share allows songwriters to upload songs, build playlists and securely share those playlists with a publisher. A publisher can then hear each song on the playlist in one page and read pertinent information on each song.

Mission-Control

Mission Control is a single page responsive CRUD web application built with JavaScript. Mission Control was designed to bring several aspects of online interaction together in one application. In Mission Control a user can manage their tasks, store articles, manage their events, add friends and allow the user to message other users. I had a role in all aspects of this project but was predominantly responsible for the events page.
